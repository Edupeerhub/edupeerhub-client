About EduPeerHub

EduPeerHub is a peer-to-peer academic support platform connecting secondary school students across Nigeria with volunteer tutors—university students, NYSC corps members, and passionate educators.

We’re on a mission to break down the barriers to quality education in underserved communities by making academic support free, accessible, and collaborative.

Whether you need help preparing for WAEC, NECO, or just need someone to explain that tough math topic, EduPeerHub is here to help you learn better, together.
