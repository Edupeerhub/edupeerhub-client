About EduPeerHub

We believe every student deserves access to quality education—regardless of where they live or how much they earn.

EduPeerHub is a peer-to-peer academic support platform connecting secondary
school students across Nigeria with volunteer tutors—university students, NYSC corps
members, and passionate educators. We’re on a mission to break down the barriers
to quality education in underserved communities by making academic support free, accessible, and collaborative. Whether you need help preparing for WAEC, NECO, or just need someone to explain that tough math topic, EduPeerHub is here to help you learn better, together.

Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to some of the most common things students and tutors ask.

What subjects are covered?

We focus on core subjects aligned with the WAEC, NECO, and JAMB curriculum — including Mathematics, English, Sciences, and more.

Who are the tutors on the platform?

Tutors are trained volunteers — university students, NYSC corps members, and experienced professionals passionate about education.

How do I become a tutor?

You can apply directly on the platform by filling out the tutor registration form.

Is EduPeerHub free to use?

Yes, EduPeerHub is completely free for students.

Can I ask questions anytime?

Absolutely! You can ask questions anytime and a tutor will respond as soon as possible.

Do I need a laptop to access EduPeerHub?

No, EduPeerHub works on smartphones, tablets, and laptops.
